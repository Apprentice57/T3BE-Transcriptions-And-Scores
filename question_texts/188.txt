OA408: What Happens When The CARES Act Expires Tomorrow?

A plaintiff sued for injuries arising from a car accident, claiming a back injury. At trial, she wishes to testify that before the accident she had never had any problems with her back.

Is the plaintiff's proposed testimony admissible?

A. No, because the plaintiff has not been qualified as an expert.
B. No, because the plaintiff's pain could have been caused by factors arising after the accident, such as an injury at work.
C. Yes, because it is probative evidence of the plaintiff's injury.
D. Yes, because the testimony of parties is not subject to the lay opinion rule.
